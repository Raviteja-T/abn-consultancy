About Us

ABN Consultancy is a forward-thinking technology firm specializing in Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and Industrial Automation. Our mission is to deliver cutting-edge solutions that transform businesses, enhance productivity, and drive sustainable growth. With a team of experienced engineers, researchers, and strategists, we bring innovation to life across industries ranging from manufacturing to logistics.

Our Services

Custom Robotics Integration

End-to-end development and deployment of bespoke robotic solutions tailored to specific industry requirements — from collaborative robots in manufacturing to autonomous platforms in logistics. We handle everything from mechanical design and sensor integration to control systems and ROS-based software development.

AI-Powered Automation Solutions

Development of intelligent automation systems leveraging computer vision, deep learning, and natural language processing. Our solutions include defect detection, predictive maintenance, autonomous decision-making, and conversational AI to enhance human-machine interaction and minimize manual intervention.

Industrial Process Optimization

Application of data analytics, simulation, and digital twin technologies to analyze, model, and optimize industrial workflows. We help organizations achieve lean operations by identifying bottlenecks, reducing energy consumption, and driving continuous improvement through real-time monitoring and feedback loops.

Training & Upskilling Programs

We offer industry-aligned professional development programs designed to empower individuals and organizations with the latest skills in robotics, AI, and automation. Our training solutions blend hands-on learning with real-world application, ensuring you stay competitive in today’s fast-evolving tech landscape.

  • Foundations of Robotics: A beginner-friendly program covering the core concepts of robotics, microcontroller programming (e.g., Arduino, Raspberry Pi), sensor integration, and the design of simple robotic systems.
  • AI & Machine Learning Bootcamps: Immersive, project-based courses focused on Python programming, machine learning fundamentals, deep learning models, and practical applications such as computer vision and natural language processing.
  • Industrial Automation Workshops: Practical training for automation engineers and technicians in PLC programming (Siemens, Allen-Bradley), sensor and actuator integration, HMI/SCADA systems, and industrial communication protocols.
  • Custom Corporate Training: Bespoke training solutions tailored to your organization’s specific technology needs. We offer on-site or remote sessions, hands-on labs, and post-training support to help your team integrate new technologies efficiently.
